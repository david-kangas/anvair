A haptic strength training timer for Apple Watch.

ANVAIR guides your rest periods through purposeful haptic cues, allowing you to stay focused on your workout instead of your watch.

Features

  • Purposeful haptic timing cues
  • Digital Crown timer adjustment
  • 30–300 second rest intervals
  • Apple Health and Fitness integration
  • No accounts
  • No subscriptions

Train by Feel

Learn the cues once and stay in your workout. ANVAIR was designed to work without requiring constant attention to the screen.
