Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

ANVAIR respects your privacy. The application does not create user accounts, collect personal information, track users, or sell or share user data.

HealthKit

ANVAIR uses Apple HealthKit to create and save workout sessions to the Apple Health app. Health and workout information remains under the control of the user and is managed by Apple Health.

ANVAIR does not transmit HealthKit data to external servers or third parties.

Data Collection

ANVAIR does not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or location information.

Third-Party Services

ANVAIR does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or third-party tracking technologies.
